Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why do all videos have a watermark?

    It is important for us, as a company that enables users to create AI-based content, that there is transparency about the synthetic nature of the videos they generate. This is also reflected in our ethical manifesto, available at http://d-id.com/ethics.

  • What are credits?

    Each credit is worth up to 15 seconds of video. When generating longer videos, credits add up according to the length of the generated video. For example, a 40-second video consumes 3 credits.

  • Do unused credits carry over to the next month?

    Credits do not accumulate, they are renewed every month and unused credits become void.

  • How can I change my credit card details?

    1. Click the menu on the bottom left and click “Billing”
    2. Click the pen icon and change the details.

  • How do I switch between the different plans?

    If you wish to upgrade or downgrade your plan, you can do that via the “Pricing” page.

  • I think I need the Enterprise plan, where can I get more information?

    For details regarding the Enterprise plan please contact our support team at support@d-id.com.

  • How do I cancel my subscription?

    To cancel your subscription, go to “Billing” > “Cancel plan”

    Important note: if you delete your account you will lose access to the studio and all your data, including your generated videos, will be deleted.

  • What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?

    If you delete your account you will lose access to the studio and all your data, including your generated videos, will be deleted.

  • How can I get an API key?

    Please go to your Accounts page in the studio, and generate your API key. Note that it is mandatory to have valid credits in your account to use the API.

  • How do you calculate my credits when I use the API?

    Credits used for the API are taken from the same balance as the studio.

  • Where can I find the API documentation?

    API documentation is available at the Developer Hub.
